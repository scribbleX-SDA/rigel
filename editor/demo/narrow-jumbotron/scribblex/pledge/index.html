Pledge for a
Better Internet.
The Internet is the most powerfull communication and collaboration resource that humans have ever witnessed. Internet has enabled new opportunities in learning, enhancing the sense of humanity, and solving problems faced globally.

Over the last few decades, we have seen Internet unleashing the good in the world. But, we have also seen the power of Internet used to magnify divisiveness, violence, hatred, and manipulate facts and reality on global levels. We've learned that we need to be more explicit about our aspirations for the human experience of the Internet, and we're doing it now.
Show Your Love, Your Support! This dream of making the Internet a better and safer place won't become a reality on its own. Individuals and Organizations must come together and embed these aspirations in technology. ScribbleX Pledge For Better Internet represents ScribbleX's commitment to advancing and enhancing these aspirations. We aim to work together with people and organizations globally who share these goals to make the internet a better and safer place for everyone and everywhere. Share on Twitter

We are committed to an Internet that encompasses all people around the world. There, individual demographics do not determine online access, ability, or quality of experience.

We are committed to an Internet that encourages critical thinking, rational debate, shared knowledge, and verifiable facts.

We are committed to an Internet that promotes civil discourse, human dignity, and individual expression.

We are committed to an Internet that fosters collaboration among diverse communities working together for the common good.
