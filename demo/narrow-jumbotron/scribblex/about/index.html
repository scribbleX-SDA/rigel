About Us




ScribbleX is a Saas (Software as a Service) startup focused on development of cloud-based web applications and technologies.

Some of the targeted areas for ScribbleX are (not limited to):


  • Development Environments
  • No-Code Builders
  • Cloud storage and computations
  • Deployment and Hosting
  • Artificial Intelligence for software and development
  • Designing and Editing platforms








Story of ScribbleX


In the year 2019, the team of ScribbleX researched the web to understand its functionality in-depth.
Our team used different platforms like:

  • Search Engines
  • Browsers
  • Programming Tools
  • Designing Tools
  • Educational Platforms
  • Social Media
  • etc...

and, we ended up finding massive voids in the platforms and services available.

ScribbleX was initiated with the goal of filling those voids and unleashing the true potential of Web Technologies.
